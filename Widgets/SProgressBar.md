### SProgressBar
#### 支持的参数

* FillType
* Percent
* ToolTipText
* Visibility

Example 1:

    "SProgressBar":
    {
        "Aka": "ProgressBarA",
        "FillType": "LeftToRight",
        "Percent": "0.5"
    }
    
#### 注意事项
Percent范围 0~1

FillType 可选项有：
- LeftToRight
- RightToLeft
- FillFromCenter
- TopToBottom
- BottomToTop
